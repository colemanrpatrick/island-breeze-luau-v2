Island Breeze Luau
“He ʻOhana Kakou”
Big Island

IslandBreezeLuau.com offers the best discounts on the Island Breeze Lū’au in Hawai’i. We are an official ticket provider for Island Breeze Lū'au in Kona and can offer you special discount rates to this popular Big Island lu’au!

Guests are invited to join the Island Breeze Lū'au in a cultural experience that shouldn't be missed. The Island Breeze Lū'au at the Courtyard Marriott King Kamehameha's Kona Beach Hotel is well known for its delicious food and its amazing Polynesian production, and location by the ocean.

Island Breeze Lū'au is the winner of the "Best of West Hawaii" award which was first presented in 2000, the Island Breeze Lū'au at the Courtyard Marriott King Kamehameha's Kona Beach Hotel is a favorite of visitors and locals.

The Island Breeze Lū'au at the Courtyard Marriott King Kamehameha’s Kona Beach Hotel begins with check-in at 5:30 pm for guests in the Hawaiian Monarchy Room. Festivities end at approximately 8:00 pm.

It is held on the beachfront of historic Kamakahonu Bay and Ahu’ena, Kamehameha the Great's former estate in Kailua-Kona. Guests of the Island Breeze Lū’au enjoy a backdrop of passing boats, outrigger canoes, and the lights of Kona and Mount Hualalai. This is one of the best Hawaii activities and an amazing lū’au.

The Island Breeze Lū'au features a delicious buffet, open bar, Polynesian hula &mo'olelo (story telling), and the Royal Court arrival. Highlighting the evening is the spectacular Polynesian Revue.

Held at the Courtyard Marriott King Kamehameha's Kona Beach Hotel, this lū’au on the Big Island of Hawai’i fully absorbs its namesake into the show. Watch as the Royal Court of King Kamehameha I arrives on stage, his arrival greeted by a Court Conch Herald and a Court Chanter. Enjoy the royal grounds to experience a lu’au that truly deserves Hawaii's Royal title.
