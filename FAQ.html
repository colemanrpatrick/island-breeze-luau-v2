Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is there wheelchair access?

    Yes.

  • Is alcohol served?

    Yes, we have a full bar. Open bar begins when luau grounds open.

  • What non-alcoholic drinks do you have?

    Standard non-alcoholic drinks including Kona Coffee, Tropical Fruit Punch, Coffee and Tea.

  • I am a vegetarian with a nut allergy, can you accommodate me?

    Yes. Special request for a vegetarian, gluten free, or celiac meal requires 24 hour notice. Option is subject to change without notice.

  • What is your dress code?

    Dress code is casual. Light cover up is suggested if you are susceptible to the cold.

  • Is it outside or inside?

    The Island Breeze Luau is outdoor.

  • Is there a dance/musical number?

    Yes, from the Fijian and Maori dances of war to the gracefulness of our Hawai'i hula hands and our daring Samoan fire-knife dance.

  • Is transportation available?

    Currently transportation is not provided.

  • Is parking available? Is parking convenient?

    Parking is available at a discounted rate (with validation) on site at the King Kamehameha Hotel.

  • Is there audience participation?

    Yes!

  • Can I take pictures after the show?

    Yes, but please no video.
